About

I recently relocated to San Diego from Boston where I was working at Wayfair as a Software Engineer, helping to manage and develop tools and resources to improve the day to day experiences of other Software Engineers. I am also working towards my Masters degree in Software Engineering. I love tackling new challenges and finding solutions to complex problems with code, and am excited to continue to work in a field that is constantly changing and growing.


My path into the world of Software Engineering began after working for a few years as a Graphic Designer. I realized I loved the problem solving and creativity involved with design but found myself more intrigued by the backend functionality of landing pages and other digital pieces. I began teaching myself how to code and was instantly hooked.


When I'm not coding, you can find me in a group fitness class, outside running, hiking or rollerblading, or working on a new crafting endeavor.
